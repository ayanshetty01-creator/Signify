How to use

  1. Open the sidebar (☰) and click a Record button.
  2. Perform the sign in front of the camera for ~2 seconds.
  3. Record 15–30 samples for each sign (better accuracy).
  4. Click Train Model, then Start Detecting.
  5. Save the model before refreshing the page.
